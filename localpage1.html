OUR MISSION

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to helping those in need. Our "Flowers for Food" campaign began after research and hands on experiences in the depths of Africa, more specfically, Tanzania and Nigeria. After many of our contributors experienced and learned more about these war-torn third world countries, they knew they needed to help somehow. This campaign supplies the people of these countries with food, water and other nutritious resources such as vitamins, dietary supplements etc. We are a by donation company, apart from ordering our beautiful flowers and flower arrangements where all the proceeds are donated to this cause, we recommend but do not require a small donation towards our organization in order for us to function properly and send our people to these countries to make sure these goods are put into the right hands.

Your flowers benefit EVERYONE!
